15.2. Aggression#

This attribute affects the forcefulness and frequency at which a player jostles and/or tackles other players.

15.3. Agility#

Out field player This attribute affects how easily and quickly a player moves and turns with the ball, especially when performing dribbles and advanced moves.

15.4. Goalkeeper#

This attribute affects the speed in which they can get up after making a save, and sets the maximum speed in which they can make a save.

15.5. Awareness#

This attribute affects the playerâ€™s ability to pick off close passes, as well as recover loose balls.

15.6. Balance#

This attribute affects how well a player can maintain their balance after a physical challenge.

15.7. Ball control#

This attribute affects how well a player initially controls the ball upon receipt, and how good they are at keeping it under control while in motion.

15.8. Crossing#

This attribute affects the speed and accuracy of a playerâ€™s cross.The crossing attribute also affects a players positioning during a cross, and players with a low crossing attribute are more likely to stick near the centre of the pitch when positioning for an attack.

15.9. Curve#

This attribute affects how much curl a player can apply on his crosses, passes and shots. This is used mostly with free kicks and finesse shots.

15.10. Dribbling#

This attribute affects how well a player can carry the ball past an opponent and how better he recovers balls from tackles.

15.11. Finishing#

This attribute affects how well the goalkeeper is at placing himself to defend against all types of shots. For example, a goalkeeper with great positioning will know when to rush towards a player in a 1 on 1, and when to stay on his line to avoid being chipped.

15.15. Handling#

15.17. Goalkeeper#

A high jumping attribute will help a goalkeeper to launch themselves farther when trying to make a leaping save.

15.18. Kicking#

This attribute affects how far a keeper can kick the ball during goal kicks and drop kicks.

15.19. Long passing#

This attribute affects the speed and accuracy of a playerâ€™s lobbed pass.

15.20. Long shots#

This attribute affects the accuracy of a shot a player makes further from the goalposts.

15.21. Marking#

This attribute affects the ability of a player to track an opponent and stay close to them to prevent him from making/receiving a pass or a cross.

15.22. Penalties#

This attribute affects the composure of a player and accuracy of their shot when taking a penalty.

15.23. Positioning#

This attribute affects the playerâ€™s ability to take up good positions on the field during a game. It also helps them be in the correct spot for a two player header battle, and determines the effectiveness of a players attacking and supporting runs.

15.24. Reactions#

Out field player This attribute affects how quickly a player reads the ball trajectory and allows him the ability to trap quick and sudden balls. It also helps a player win two player headers, and lets defenders perform faster clearances and tackles.

15.25. Goalkeeper#

This attribute affects how quickly a goalkeeper launches a save animation.

15.26. Reflexes#

This attribute affects how quickly a goalkeeper reacts to shots or deflections. A goalkeeper with high Reflex attribute will also be faster when trying to dive at a playerâ€™s feet to strip the ball off them.

This attribute affects the accuracy of a playerâ€™s ground pass (long and shot) and the speed at which they launch passes.A high Short Passing attribute also enables fancy passes, like back-heel passes. 15.29. Sliding Tackle#
